$a^2 = (b - c * cosθ)^2 + (C * sinθ)^2$

$= b^2 - 2bc*cosθ + c^2(cosθ)^2 + c^2(sinθ)^2$

$= b^2 + c^2(cos^2θ + sin^2θ) - 2bc*cosθ$

$cos^2θ + sin^2θ = 1$ 이므로

$a^2 = b^2 + c^2 - 2bc*cosθ$
